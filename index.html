The James Webb Space Telescope

The James Webb Space Telescope (JWST) represents a monumental leap forward in space exploration and astronomy. Scheduled for launch, JWST promises to revolutionize our understanding of the cosmos by peering deeper into space and time than ever before. With its advanced instruments, JWST aims to uncover the secrets of the early universe, study the formation of galaxies, and even investigate the potential for life beyond Earth.
